# SHADOW

**Face what you've been avoiding.**

A dark, private PWA for shadow work — the Jungian practice of confronting the parts of yourself you've buried. No accounts. No cloud. No one watching. Just you and the void.

**[Enter the shadow &rarr;](https://shadow-work-app.netlify.app)**

---

## What is this?

Shadow work is the process of making the unconscious conscious. The traits you deny, the emotions you suppress, the patterns you repeat — they don't disappear. They run you from the dark.

This app gives you structure to face them.

## Features

- **Daily Check-In** — A 4-step ritual. Rate your presence. Name the emotion. Track the trigger. Write what surfaced.
- **Guided Exercises** — Shadow Journaling, 3-2-1 Process, Inner Child Work, Active Imagination, Mirror Work, Trigger Tracking, Dream Work. Categorized by depth: _Surface → Deep → Abyss_.
- **Journal** — Freeform writing with prompts designed to pull things out of you.
- **Dream Journal** — Track the figures and emotions your unconscious sends you at night.
- **Trigger Patterns** — Log what sets you off. See the pattern. Break it.
- **Progress** — Streaks, heatmaps, stage progression. Not gamification — accountability.

## Privacy

Everything stays on your device. IndexedDB. No server. No analytics. No telemetry. Your shadow is yours.

## Stack

React 19 · TypeScript · Vite · Dexie (IndexedDB) · Framer Motion · PWA · CSS Modules

## Install

```bash
cd shadow-work
npm install
npm run dev
```

Or just use it at **[shadow-work-app.netlify.app](https://shadow-work-app.netlify.app)** — it installs as a PWA on your phone.
